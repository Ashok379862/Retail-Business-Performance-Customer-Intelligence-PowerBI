# Retail Business Performance & Customer Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Power BI solution designed to analyze retail business performance with a strong focus on sales, profitability, and customer intelligence.  
The dashboards are built to support executive-level decision-making using clean visuals, well-structured data modeling, and optimized DAX measures.

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Power Query  
- Star Schema Data Modeling  

---

##  Dashboards Included

### 1 Executive Overview
- Total Sales, Orders, Profit, Profit Margin, Average Order Value
- Regional contribution to total sales
- Revenue share by product category
- Monthly sales trend analysis

### 2 Profitability Analysis
- Profit contribution by category
- Profit & loss analysis by sub-category
- Sales vs Profit scatter analysis to identify high-risk and high-opportunity products

### 3 Customer Intelligence
- Total customers and average revenue per customer
- Top customers by revenue
- Revenue distribution by customer segment
- Repeat vs one-time customer analysis

### 4 Time Trend & Forecast
- Sales trend over time
- Forecast visualization to understand future sales direction

---

##  Data Modeling
- Implemented a star schema with fact and dimension tables
- Separate dimensions for Date, Customers, Products, and Geography
- Optimized relationships for accurate filtering and performance

---

##  Key Business Insights
- Identified top-performing regions and categories
- Highlighted loss-making sub-categories impacting profitability
- Analyzed customer retention and revenue concentration
- Evaluated long-term sales trends for strategic planning
